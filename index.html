Orange

April 2, 2014 by GqYYPYUIr9

the DEEP WEB TOUR started.

oakland was hard because we didn’t play.  which was weird.  people were mad.  but then someone got married, rather engaged, outside so people got happy again and forgave.  it was the computer’s fault tho.

we talked about getting a tour pet.  stefan is unofficially the tour pet.

it’s very cool to see people use the DEEP WEB app. people draw dicks and swastikas.

in santa barbara, we got to play outside.  i had a burrito for breakfast that morning. it was fun cause fam and i wore backpacks to blend in to the crowd on campus to get there.  someone said “that nigga think he childish gambino”. i did think that.

when we get to stop at a hotel,  we call down and tell the concierge to check on our children at 9pm because “we’re going out to a cocktail party and they should be in bed by that time”. and when they go up there, steve and swank (two grown men who are probably smoking blunts and listening to young scooter) are up there. the reaction is always enjoyable.  we’re trying to find an older white couple to come downstairs and tell the concierge for us, to really sell it. but we haven’t found someone willing to yet.

 

“you’re gonna miss every moment of this…of everything.”

sometimes i feel like i’m already gone/ahead and i’m just vividly remembering everything that’s happening in the moment. fur real. sometimes everything feels too familiar to be just happening for the first time. the shows feel like that sometimes. you can’t understand something totally and enjoy it at the same time, i think. but it’s fun to not know all of it.  everybody wants some magic.  something they can’t quite figure out.

someone put girl scout cookies in the freezer. hot damn.
